US Bookkeeping Services

Done-for-you QuickBooks Online Bookkeeping

I provide remote bookkeeping support for US small businesses and CPA firms using QuickBooks Online and Excel-based automation, backed by 20+ years of accounting experience. My goal is to keep your books accurate, tax-ready, and easy for both you and your CPA to understand.

👉 Ready to clean up your QuickBooks file or start monthly bookkeeping?
Use the contact form or send me a WhatsApp message to schedule a free 20–30 minute call.

Who I Work With

  • US-based small businesses using QuickBooks Online
  • E-commerce sellers, online service providers and consultants
  • CPA / EA firms needing reliable back-office bookkeeping support
  • Solo founders and side-hustlers who don’t have time for bookkeeping

Services

Clean-up & Catch-up
  • Review existing QBO file and identify issues
  • Import and reconcile missing bank / credit card transactions
  • Fix mis-categorised expenses and income
  • Clear old unreconciled and duplicate entries
  • Make your books tax-ready for CPA / EA
Ongoing Monthly Bookkeeping
  • Monthly / weekly transaction categorisation
  • Bank, credit card and payment processor reconciliations
  • Accounts receivable and payable tracking (basic AR / AP)
  • Monthly Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet and Cash Flow reports
  • Email summary or call to review key numbers

How It Works

  1. Short call / WhatsApp chat: understand your business and goals.
  2. QBO file review: I review your QuickBooks Online and bank statements.
  3. Scope & quote: you get a simple proposal (one-time clean-up and / or monthly fee).
  4. Onboarding: secure access sharing and initial set-up / corrections.
  5. Ongoing work: regular bookkeeping, reconciliations and monthly reporting.

Why Work With Me

  • 20+ years of hands-on accounting and tax experience
  • QuickBooks Online Level 1 certified (ProAdvisor track)
  • Based in India with lower costs while serving US businesses remotely
  • You work directly with me – not an anonymous team
  • Easy communication via WhatsApp and email

Pricing Approach

Pricing depends on transaction volume, number of bank / credit card accounts, and complexity of your business. In general, I offer:

  • Fixed-fee clean-up projects for past months / years
  • Monthly retainers for ongoing bookkeeping
  • Custom arrangements for CPA / EA firms with multiple end-clients

Share a brief outline of your business and bookkeeping situation, and I’ll respond with a clear, no-obligation estimate within 24 hours.
