About Riveda Infosoft

An Accounting Technology Company — desktop software, automation, and accounting services for businesses worldwide.

Who We Are

Riveda Infosoft is an Accounting Technology Company that develops desktop business software, accounting automation tools, and cloud integrations for Chartered Accountants, tax professionals, and growing businesses. We combine over 20 years of hands-on accounting experience with practical software development using QuickBooks Online, Tally Prime, Excel, VBA, and the Meta WhatsApp Business Cloud API.

In addition to software products, we provide US bookkeeping services and Indian accounting services — helping clients keep their books clean, compliant, and efficient.

Desktop Software

Windows desktop applications including Smart CA Communicator, Smart Bank Import Suite, Tally School and College Soft, Tally Society Module, and Tally Hospital Module — built for accountants and businesses.

Automation

Business and accounting automation — bank statement import, GST reconciliation, document conversion, and workflow tools that reduce manual data entry and errors.

Cloud Integrations

WhatsApp Business Cloud API integration via Meta Embedded Signup, QuickBooks Online bookkeeping, and Tally Prime connectivity for modern accounting workflows.

Professional Accounting Services

Riveda Infosoft provides accounting support, bookkeeping assistance, Tally automation consulting, and practical workflow support for businesses and professionals where required. Our main focus remains accounting technology, desktop software, and automation solutions.

  • Tally automation consulting and workflow support
  • Accounting and bookkeeping assistance, including US QuickBooks Online support where required
  • Monthly bookkeeping and bank / credit card reconciliations
  • 6–24+ months catch-up and clean-up projects
  • QBO setup / re-setup and chart of accounts structuring
  • Back-office support for CPAs and EA firms

Tally, Software & Automation (India)

For Indian businesses using Tally Prime, we develop desktop software and practical automation solutions that reduce manual work and improve accuracy.

  • Smart CA Communicator – WhatsApp Business desktop app for CAs
  • Smart CA Communicator, Smart Bank Import Suite, Tally School and College Soft, Tally Society Module, Tally Hospital Module
  • Custom TDL reports, Tally modules, and system integrations
  • Support for SMEs, societies, schools, hospitals and professionals
