About Riveda Infosoft

Reliable back-office bookkeeping support for US CPA and EA firms

Who We Are

Riveda Infosoft provides remote bookkeeping and accounting support to US-based CPA and EA firms, as well as small and mid-sized businesses using QuickBooks Online.

The practice is led by a senior accounting professional with over 20 years of hands-on experience in bookkeeping, reconciliations, cleanup projects, and financial reporting.

Our role is simple: operate as a quiet, reliable extension of your firm so you can focus on advisory work, tax planning, and client relationships.

How We Support CPA & EA Firms

  • Monthly bookkeeping and bank / credit card reconciliations
  • Catch-up and clean-up projects (6–24+ months)
  • Review and correction of mis-categorized transactions
  • Preparation of CPA-ready financial statements
  • Overflow support during tax season

We follow your firm’s workflows, chart of accounts, and documentation standards. All work is confidential and fully review-ready.

Why Firms Choose Riveda Infosoft

  • Experience-first approach – not junior outsourcing
  • CPA-ready work – minimal rework required
  • Clear communication aligned with US time zones
  • Confidential & process-driven engagement
  • No client-facing risk – we stay in the background

Technology & Tools

We primarily work with:

  • QuickBooks Online (Setup, Cleanup, Reconciliation)
  • Structured Excel-based data review
  • Process automation tools where appropriate

Automation is used only when it improves accuracy and audit readiness — never at the cost of control.
