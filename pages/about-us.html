About Riveda Infosoft

Helping US and Indian businesses with clean books and practical automation.

Who We Are

Riveda Infosoft is a boutique accounting and automation practice helping businesses keep their books clean, compliant, and efficient. We combine over 20 years of hands-on accounting experience with practical automation using QuickBooks Online, Tally Prime, Excel, and VBA.

US Online Bookkeeping

For US-based small businesses and CPA / EA firms, we provide remote bookkeeping on QuickBooks Online. The focus is on maintaining accurate, tax-ready books that your CPA can trust.

  • Monthly bookkeeping and bank / credit card reconciliations
  • 6–24+ months catch-up and clean-up projects
  • QBO setup / re-setup and chart of accounts structuring
  • Back-office support for CPAs and EA firms

Tally & Automation Solutions (India)

For Indian businesses using Tally Prime, we design practical automation solutions that reduce manual work and improve accuracy.

  • Custom TDL reports and Tally modules
  • PDF to Excel Converter, GST EasyMatch, TallyBridge, Tally-Society Connect
  • Integration between Tally and other systems
  • Support for SMEs, societies, schools, hospitals and professionals

About the Founder – Varadha Raju

Riveda Infosoft is led by Varadha Raju, an accountant and educator with over 20 years of experience in accounting, taxation, and teaching. With a strong background in Tally, Excel, VBA and financial automation, he has helped Indian SMEs streamline their finance functions and now supports US small businesses remotely on QuickBooks Online.

After years of working with educational institutions, SMEs, and professionals, Varadha now focuses on remote US bookkeeping and practical automation that saves time for accountants and business owners.

How We Work

  • Listen first: understand your business, systems, and pain points.
  • Diagnose: review your QuickBooks Online file or Tally setup to identify issues.
  • Propose: share a clear scope and pricing (project-based or monthly).
  • Execute: fix, automate, and maintain with regular communication.
  • Support: ongoing help via WhatsApp, email, and scheduled calls.

Who We Serve

  • US service businesses, freelancers, e-commerce sellers and CPA / EA firms using QuickBooks Online
  • Indian SMEs, societies, schools / colleges, hospitals and professionals using Tally Prime
